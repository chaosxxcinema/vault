## What is this?

Your skincare, your supplements, your camera, that candle you keep rebuying —
when you started using it, when you ran out, when you switched brands, when it finally retired…

These small moments slip through the cracks. Notes apps can't hold them. Memory fades.

**VAULT turns each item into a little cell with its own timeline.** Snap a photo, jot a line, and it remembers for you.
Open it later and it reads like flipping through an old album — not a list, but slices of your own life.

---

## How it works

> **Snap it. Note it. Replace it when the time comes. It's all still there.**

1. 📷 **Snap** — see something worth keeping, take a photo
2. 🗂️ **Sort** — drop it into a vault (skincare, supplements, gear… your categories)
3. ⏳ **Look back** — repurchases, upgrades, retirements all stack into one timeline

VAULT opens with a **sample skincare vault** holding two years of one person's real routine — so you can see what it's like before you start your own.

---

## Features

- 🕯️ **A timeline, not a list** — every item carries its own history, warm to revisit
- 🔒 **Your data is yours** — local-first; everything stays on your device. **No cloud, no snooping.**
- 🔍 **Search anything** — vaults, items, brands, tags, notes — just type
- ⏰ **Replacement reminders** — set a date for things running low, never forget
- 🎨 **Feels like a journal** — warm paper textures, custom covers and colors
- 📥 **Back up anytime** — one-tap export, switch phones without losing a thing

---

## Privacy promise

VAULT collects nothing.
The fragments of your life belong to you — not to any tech giant.
